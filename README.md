📖 Project Overview

This repository contains my Digital Literacy Portfolio Project, completed as part of the CSE0001 course.

In this project, I took on the role of a Student Digital Ambassador, responsible for spreading awareness about digital tools, online communication, and cyber safety among peers.

The project focuses on developing practical digital skills that are essential for academic success and professional growth in today’s digital world.

🎯 Objectives

To understand the concept of digital literacy

To build a professional online presence

To learn effective digital communication

To explore coding and collaboration platforms

To gain awareness about cybercrime and online safety

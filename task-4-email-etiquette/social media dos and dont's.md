Do’s and Don’ts of social media
 Do’s
1. Think before you post
Make sure your content is respectful, accurate, and appropriate.
2. Protect your privacy
Use strong passwords, enable privacy settings, and avoid sharing personal details like address or phone number.
3. Be respectful and kind
Engage positively with others and avoid offensive language or behavior.
4. Verify information before sharing
Check the credibility of news or posts to avoid spreading misinformation.
5. Build a positive digital footprint
Share content that reflects your skills, interests, and values.
6. Use social media productively
Follow educational pages, learn new skills, and stay informed.
7. Credit original creators
Give proper attribution when sharing someone else's content.

 
 Don’ts
1. Don’t overshare personal information
Avoid posting sensitive details that could compromise your safety.
2. Don’t engage in cyberbullying
Never harass, threaten, or insult others online.
3. Don’t spread fake news or rumors
Sharing unverified information can harm others and damage your credibility.
4. Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content
It can negatively impact your reputation and future opportunities.
5. Don’t get addicted
Limit screen time to maintain a healthy balance with real life.
6. Don’t click on suspicious links
They may lead to scams, phishing, or malware.
7. Don’t argue aggressively online
Heated arguments can escalate quickly—stay calm or disengage.
